Craving something sweet and salty, and you are a fan of cashews! Then this is the recipe for you. Letâ€™s make these utterly delicious, lightly salted cashews at home. All you need is salt and cashews, toss them and roast them in a pan, until light golden brown.

## Ingredients

- Cashew nuts 
- 1.5 Cups Salt

## Method

1. Heat a kadhai over high flame; add salt and heat up the salt. Keep stirring the salt with a perforated spoon.
2. Once the salt is hot enough, lower the flame to medium and add the cashew nuts.
3. Start stirring continuously for about a minute or till they turn golden brown. 
4. Remove them using a slotted spoon and transfer them in a large plate. Allow the roasted salted cashew nuts to cool down completely.
5. Once they come to room temperature, mix it with your hands, to get rid of the excess salt.
6. Serve the salted cashew nuts as is 

NOTE:
- You can use the same salt, again to roast other nuts.
- Stirring continuously is the key to a good roasted cashew nuts.
